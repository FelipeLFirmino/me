MSPA is a software platform that helps companies implement LGPD (Brazil's General Data Protection Law) compliance. I contributed as a fullstack developer across every major part of the product: the Angular/TypeScript frontend, the Golang REST API backend, and later led development as Product Lead — covering Stripe payment integration, Hexagonal Architecture (Ports and Adapters), and Domain-Driven Design.

The link above points to the public marketing site, which I also helped build. The compliance software itself is a paid product and isn't publicly accessible, so it isn't shown here.
